ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Gaston Jarju, pronounced as “Gas-tin”.I am currently in my final year at Berea college, Kentucky, pursuing a Computer Science major and a minor in Business Administration. At my college, I participate in the Labor Program as a student photographer, where I specialize in portrait photography, including studio portraits and outdoor event photography.The images I capture connect the college to the outside audience like alumni, donors, prospective students, and other key stakeholders.

Outside college photography, I love to go to locations and shoot street and infrastructure photography. I am also open to working with other photographers and models, so please don't hesitate to get in touch with me to discuss your photography wishes.
